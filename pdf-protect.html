How to Protect Your PDF

  1. Upload your PDF

    Click the upload button or drag and drop your PDF file into the designated area.

  2. Set a password

    Enter a strong password and confirm it. This will be required to open the PDF.

  3. Choose permissions

    Select what restrictions you want to apply to the protected PDF.

  4. Download protected PDF

    Click "Protect PDF" and download your secured document.
